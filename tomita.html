Detailed Report

The Expand Algorithm by E. Tomita et al. is an efficient method for enumerating all maximal cliques in a graph. Below are the key observations from our experiments:

  • Largest Clique Size: 20 (Email-Enron), 17 (Wiki-Vote), 67 (AS-Skitter)
  • Total Maximal Cliques: 226859 (Email-Enron), 459002 (Wiki-Vote), 37322355 (AS-Skitter)
  • Execution Time: 7.257 (Email-Enron), 4.844 (Wiki-Vote), (AS-Skitter)
